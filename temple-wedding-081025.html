Temple Wedding Detailed Schedule

Date: 10-Oct-25

17:00 - Edurukola

  • The bride’s family formally welcomes the groom’s party.

19:00 - Main Wedding Ceremony

  • Gauri Puja: Bride prays to Goddess Gauri (Parvati).
  • Ganapati Puja: Invocation of Lord Ganesha.
  • Vara Puja: Honoring the groom as the chosen partner.
  • Kanyadanam: Bride’s parents entrust her to the groom.
  • Jeelakarra-Bellam (Muhurtham - 20:24): Couple place cumin–jaggery paste on each other’s heads, symbolizing inseparable union.
  • Madhuparkalu: Offering of honey and milk.
  • Bride enters with Jyothulu: Entry with sacred lamps.
  • Mangalya Dharana: Groom ties the mangalsutra.
  • Talambralu: Couple shower turmeric rice on each other.

Concluding Vedic Rituals (Yagashala)

  • Nagavalli Homam: Fire ritual with betel leaves.
  • Sthalipakam: Groom places toe rings on bride’s feet.
  • Sadasyam: Formal witnessing by elders.
  • Saptapadi: Seven vows around the sacred fire.
  • Arundhati Nakshatra Darsanam: Couple view Arundhati star.
  • Appagintalu: Bride is welcomed into groom’s family.
