Upanayanam Schedule

Date: 10-Oct-25

The upanayanam marks the sacred initiation of the groom. During this ceremony he receives the sacred thread (yajnopavita) and is symbolically admitted into Vedic learning and ritual life. It signifies purity, discipline, and readiness to take on spiritual and household responsibilities.

  • 08:30 — Breakfast: Guests are served before the ceremonies begin.
  • 09:30 — Kumara Bhojanam: Children are fed first in a special meal. This act symbolizes prosperity, the blessing of future generations, and continuity of lineage in the family.
  • 11:14 — Snatakam: Conducted at an auspicious muhurtham, this is a “graduation” rite where the groom symbolically completes his student life and prepares to enter the stage of a householder. It signifies the groom’s readiness to take on responsibilities of family and society. The rituals conclude with Lunch at 1:00 pm.
