Upanayanam Schedule

Date: 10-Oct-25

The upanayanam marks the spiritual initiation of the groom, where he is given the sacred thread (yajnopavita) and formally begins his journey into Vedic learning and ritual duties. It symbolizes purity, discipline, and readiness for marriage.

  • 8:30 am — Breakfast: Guests are served before rituals begin.
  • 9:30 am — Kumara Bhojanam: Children (especially boys) are fed first, representing prosperity and continuity of lineage.
  • 11:14 am — Snatakam: Ritual “graduation” where the groom completes student life and prepares for married life. Concludes with lunch at 1 pm.
